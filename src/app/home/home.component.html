Coronavirus (Covid-19) is a virus causing a new global pandemic. Our best defense is to self-isolate to protect our most susceptible.

Watch for Symptoms of Coronavirus

Reported illnesses have ranged from mild symptoms to severe illness and death confirmed coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) cases.

These symptoms may appear 2-14 days after after exposure (based on the incubation period of MERS-CoV viruses):

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath

When to Seek Medical Attention

If you develop the emergency warning signs for COVID-19 get medical attention immediately. Emergency signs include*:

  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • New confusion or inability to arouse
  • Bluish lips or face

*This is not all inclusive. Please consult your medical provider for any other symptoms that are severe or converning.
